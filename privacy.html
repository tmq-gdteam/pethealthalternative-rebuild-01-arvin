Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pet Health Alternatives ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect through our website, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website or submitting one of our forms, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information you choose to share with us:

  • •Contact & appointment forms: your name, email address, phone number, and the message you send us.
  • •Newsletter signup: your email address.

When you submit a form on this site, it is sent directly to the practice's email handler so we can respond to you. Submitting a contact or appointment form means you agree to be contacted by us about your request.

How We Use It

We use the information you provide to:

  • •Respond to your questions and contact you about an appointment.
  • •Provide and coordinate the veterinary care you request.
  • •Send you holistic pet care tips and updates, if you subscribed to our newsletter.

How We Share It

We do not sell your data. We do not rent or trade your personal information. We may share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate this website or communicate with you (such as our email or form-handling provider), and only as needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to help the site function and to understand how visitors use it. If we use analytics tools, they collect general, non-identifying usage information such as pages visited and approximate location. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

Data Retention

We keep the information you submit only as long as needed to respond to your request, provide care, meet our recordkeeping obligations, or as required by law. When it is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or secure it.

Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly. You may also ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have shared with us, and we will respond as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, please reach out:

  • •Call or text: 727-753-9358
  • •Email: PetHealthAlternatives@gmail.com

This policy is provided as a general template and should be reviewed by a qualified professional before publication.
